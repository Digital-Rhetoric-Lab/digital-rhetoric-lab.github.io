Step 1: Idea generation

Find three meaningful moments from reading and writing in digital environments.

Prompt:

Reflect on your experiences reading and writing in digital environments. Identify three moments where you learned something important about how digital networks function and how you engage with them. As you describe each moment, include specific details so your reader can clearly see what happened.

Step 2: Connect to digital literacy dimensions

Tag each moment and explain how it demonstrates one or more dimensions of digital literacy.

Prompt:

How does this moment demonstrate one or more dimensions of digital literacy?

Step 3: Narrative development

Expand each moment into a detailed narrative paragraph that shows both what you did and what you learned.

Prompt:

Expand this moment to a detailed narrative paragraph that shows both what you wrote/read and what you learned.

Step 4: Full digital literacy narrative

Bring your three moments together into a cohesive essay draft.

Prompt:

Bring your three moments together into a cohesive digital literacy narrative. Your essay should show how your digital literacy has developed across different situations.
